KIDNAPING



Rubén Figueroa Alcocer
Call: 1-800-616-1600

The FBI and the Dirección Federal de Seguridad (DFS) are currently investigating the disappearance of RUBÉN FIGUEROA ALCOCER.

On Monday, May 30, 1966, at approximately 7:00 pm, 74-year old RUBÉN FIGUEROA ALCOCER was abducted in the sierra of the Costa Grande region of Guerrero. He had been travelling in the Guerrero region as part of his electoral campaign for state governorship, and had been in the midst of negotiations with the revolutionary guerilla organization known as the Party of the Poor (PDLP). He has since been nominated as a candidate for governor of Guerrero in the weeks since his kidnapping. Mexican authorities suspect Lucio Cabañas and the PDLP to be responsible for FIGUEROA’s disappearance, and have advised local residents to report any revolutionary activity to state officials as soon as possible.

FIGUEROA is an older Hispanic male, 5 feet 6 inches tall, and weighs approximately 170 pounds. He has a light complexion, short dark brown hair, and a scar above his left eye. He was wearing a white collared shirt, blue jeans and brownish-yellow boots at the time of his disappearance.

Anyone who has seen FIGUEROA, or who has any information about his disappearance is encouraged to contact the Dirección Federal de Seguridad at 1-800-616-1600 and ask for Miguel Nazar Haro.
